Features

Built for the drive

🎚️

Five custom tiers

Dial in a target volume for each speed band — from a city crawl to highway cruising — exactly how you like it.

🌊

Smooth transitions

Volume eases between tiers instead of jumping, with adjustable sensitivity to match your driving style.

⌚

Wear OS companion

Tune tiers and stop the service straight from your watch — full parity with the phone, no need to reach for it.

Runs in the background

Dark & light themes

How it works

Three steps to set it

1

Set your tiers

Choose a target volume for each of the five speed thresholds and pick your sensitivity.

2

Tap to activate

Start the service and put the phone down. FlowAudio takes over from there.

3

Just drive

As your speed changes, your volume follows — automatically and smoothly.

Control it from your wrist

The Wear OS companion mirrors your phone: adjust each tier's volume, change sensitivity, see whether the service is active, and stop it instantly — all without taking your hands off the wheel for longer than a glance.

⚠️

Safety first

Set your volume tiers so you can always hear sirens, horns, and traffic at every speed. Configure FlowAudio before you set off — never while driving. Stay aware of your surroundings at all times.
